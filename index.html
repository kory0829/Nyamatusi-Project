Our Story

Nyamatusi is our flagship in terms of its Eco-conscious style.Our Mana Pools safari lodge is one of the wildest and most remote parts of Africa, on the banks of the Zambezi , one of the world is oldest rivers. Embracing comfort, this property embodies sunstainable dsign. Fully solar-powered and built from all-natural materials it encompasses its wilderness location in the most elegant way.Here your Mana pools Safari plays out against a sweeping riverine landscape, where epic encounters of the giants that roam this land wait.
